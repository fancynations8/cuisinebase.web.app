The ingredients needed to make Diet Caesar Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 head romaine lettuce, cut into bite-size pieces
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup croutons
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  4. Prepare Dressing:
  5. Take 4 tablespoons olive oil
  6. Prepare 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  7. Take 2 tablespoons red vinegar
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  10. Take 1 clove garlic, crushed
  11. Get 3 flat anchovies, minced (optional)
Steps to make Diet Caesar Salad:
  1. In a bowl, mix the lemon juice, vinegar, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, anchovies if using, salt and pepper until well-combined.
  2. Slowly add in the olive oil in a steady flow while constantly whisking until the dressing is smooth.
  3. In a salad bowl, add in the lettuce and half the dressing and toss. Then add the remaining dressing along with the parmesan cheese, and croutons and toss again.
