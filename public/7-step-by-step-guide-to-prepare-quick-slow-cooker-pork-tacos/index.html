The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Pork Tacos:
  1. Take 5-6 lbs Pork Shoulder (bone in preferred)
  2. Make ready 4 cups Beef Broth
  3. Get 2 cups water
  4. Get 2 Large Onions (quartered or julienne)
  5. Prepare 4 Poblano peppers (julienne)
  6. Take 2 Jalapenos (diced)
  7. Get 2 tbsp Parsley
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp Basil
  9. Make ready 4 tbsp Knorr Chicken Bouillon
  10. Get Queso fresco
  11. Make ready Mexican sour cream
  12. Prepare Corn Tortillas (flour if preferred)

Steps to make Slow Cooker Pork Tacos:
  1. Put pork shoulder in roaster. Add all other ingredients. Set roaster to 225 degrees.
  2. After cooking for 12.5 hours shred for tacos.
