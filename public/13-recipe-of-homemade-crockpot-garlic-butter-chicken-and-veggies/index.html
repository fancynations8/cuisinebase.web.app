The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Garlic Butter Chicken and Veggies:
  1. Prepare Bag Baby Carrots
  2. Make ready 5 Red Potatoes, cubed
  3. Prepare 1 Yellow Onion, sliced
  4. Get 1 Zucchini, cubed
  5. Prepare 2-3 Lg. Chicken breasts, boneless and skinless cut into strips
  6. Make ready 2 Sticks Salted Butter
  7. Prepare 1 tsp. Onion Powder
  8. Make ready 1-2 tsp. Minced Garlic
  9. Get 1 Tbsp. Dried Cilantro
  10. Make ready 1 Tbsp. Dried Tarragon
  11. Prepare 1 tsp. All-purpose Seasoning

Instructions to make Crockpot Garlic Butter Chicken and Veggies:
  1. In a small saucepan add the butter and seasonings and minced garlic. Cook on low til butter is melted and set aside.
  2. Add chicken to crockpot then then the rest of ingredients. Pour the butter sauce over everything. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours or on low for 8 hours.
