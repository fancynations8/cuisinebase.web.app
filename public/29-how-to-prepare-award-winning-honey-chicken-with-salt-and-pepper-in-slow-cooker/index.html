Instructions to make Honey Chicken with Salt and Pepper in Slow Cooker:
  1. Pat dry the chicken. Pour oil into chicken and make sure it coats the chicken perfectly.
  2. Mix honey with salt and pepper. Coat the chicken with this mixture.
  3. Insert onion into the chicken. Cut the butter and carefully slip some underneath the skin and inside the chicken.
  4. Cook HIGH for 3-4 hours or LOW for 5-6 hours.
