The ingredients needed to make Crock pot botwie casserole:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 lbs Italian sausage
  2. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  3. Prepare 1 choice garlic minced
  4. Take 15 oz can diced tomatoes
  5. Make ready 1 tsp oregano
  6. Get 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  7. Make ready 10 oz fresh spinach
  8. Take 16 oz bowtie pasta cooked
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 cup mozzarella

Steps to make Crock pot botwie casserole:
  1. Brown Italian sausage
  2. Add sausage, tomatoes, and seasoning into crockpot. Cook 6-7 hours on low
  3. Add pasta, parmesan and 1 cup mozzarella cheese during the last 30 minutes and turn crockpot on high.
  4. Add fresh spinach with 5 minutes left, once wilted add rest of mozzarella cheese on top
