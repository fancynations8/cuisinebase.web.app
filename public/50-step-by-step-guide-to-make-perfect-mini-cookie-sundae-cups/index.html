Instructions to make Mini cookie sundae cups:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray a muffin pan with cooking spray.
  2. Press 1-2 squares of cookie dough in each cup in the muffin pan and bake 8-10 minutes until edges are golden brown.
  3. Cool 30 minutes then remove using a butter knife to go around the edges and pry them out.
  4. Put as many scoops of ice cream in them as you want and add on the toppings!
