The ingredients needed to make Caesar Salad:
  1. Take Romaine lettuce, leaves separated and cut
  2. Prepare Cherry tomatoes (quartered)
  3. Get Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  4. Take Dressing
  5. Prepare 1 Egg yolk
  6. Prepare 125 ml Cream
  7. Take 50 ml Olive oil
  8. Prepare 1 Garlic (minced)
  9. Make ready 25 g Anchovy
  10. Make ready 20 g Sherry vinegar
  11. Prepare Salt
  12. Get Pepper
  13. Take Croutons
  14. Take 2 slices Bread
  15. Get Butter
  16. Take Salt
Instructions to make Caesar Salad:
  1. Croutons : cut the bread into cubes, toss the bread cubes with butter, and salt. Spread evenly on oven and bake at 100 degree Celsius for 50 min or until crispy
  2. Dressing : whisk all dressing ingredients and season with salt and pepper, keep cool in the refrigerator
  3. Parmesan crisp : spread the grated Parmesan cheese on a baking sheet and bake in oven at 150 degree Celsius for 15 min or until cheese melt and slightly crispy, cheese crisp will turn crispier when cool
  4. In a large mixing bowl, toss gently the lettuce with the Caesar dressing until the lettuce coated with the dressing, season with some salt and pepper
  5. Top with the cherry tomatoes, bread croutons and Parmesan cheese crisp
  6. Alternatively, you can present it cutely like this to impress your guests, enjoy 😋
