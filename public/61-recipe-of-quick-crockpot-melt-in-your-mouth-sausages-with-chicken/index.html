The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Melt In Your Mouth Sausages with Chicken 🐔:
  1. Prepare 1 large family pack Hot Italian sausages
  2. Prepare 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  3. Prepare 1 jar your favorite pasta sauce
  4. Get 10 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Make ready 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon parsley flakes
  7. Get 1/4 teaspoon adobo seasoning
  8. Take 1 cup water
  9. Get 1 green pepper, sliced
  10. Take 1 onion, sliced
  11. Get 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
Steps to make Crockpot Melt In Your Mouth Sausages with Chicken 🐔:
  1. Place sausages in a pot with enough water to cover them, boil for about 30 minutes…..
  2. Remove sausages from pot and let cool, when your able to comfortably handle them remove casings from sausages, discard casing and cut sausages into thirds, place into crockpot…….
  3. Place cut up chicken chunks into crockpot with sausages……Mix well……
  4. Combine in crockpot: pasta sauce, garlic, tomato paste, parsley flakes, adobo, water, green pepper and onion, mix all well…..
  5. Cook, covered on low heat for 6 hours……. Stir occasionally…….
  6. Serve and enjoy 😉! You can serve with your favorite pasta with Parmesan cheese or on top of some toasted buns !!! 😁
